Below are steps to find listings on XMR Bazaar and purchase product via Amazon with fiat to receive non-KYC XMR. You only need to purchase the item and supply tracking information. Once the item is delivered, you'll receive the agreed XMR amount.

  1. Search XMR Bazaar for items to purchase on Amazon
  2. Accept Offer
  3. Purchase Items. Check "this is a gift" and ship to the shoppers whishlist address
  4. When item ships, get tracking number and post on XMR Bazaar
  5. Wait for item delivery and receive XMR

This is a bookmarklet to extract tracking numbers from Amazon tracking pages.

Right click this link Get Tracking Number and add to your bookmarks.

Then visit your Amazon tracking page and select this bookmarklet (from your bookmarks).
