Common Questions

Here are some questions I had while building this guide. If you have more questions or suggestions, you can post on the Github Project.

General Questions

Why?
I was a huge fan of the now defunct Purse(.io) where you could purchase items on Amazon at a discount with BTC/BCH. While others could earn non-KYC BTC & BCH by purchasing items with fiat.

I believe Purse started as a case study, that turned into a valuable way to transact with crypto. It promoted the use of crypto instead of just hodling.

I've been searching for a Purse alternative since they shut down in early 2023. Everything I found felt "sketchy" and/or had extremely high fees. XMR Bazaar is the perfect place to match shoppers & earners.

I've completed 5 Amazon purchases using XMR Bazaar. Received a 10% discount (at the time) and the Earner has gained 72% since then. A win-win for both parties.

I've written this guide to describe how I did it. I hope to help integrate Amazon Wishlists into XMR Bazaar to make it easier & more popular in the future.

Is this totally anonymous?
No.

If a shopper wants additional anonynimity, they can ship to a 3rd party mailbox, or use an Amazon Locker/Pickup location.

Could XMR Bazaar become an Amazon affiliate to earn commissions?
I don't think so.

The Amazon Affiliate program only pays commissions for "qualifying purchases". They only allow direct purchases via an affiliate link. I don't believe it covers purchases for other people (wishlists), or random items without an affiliate link.

You can read more here https://affiliate-program.amazon.com/help/node


Shopper Questions

Can I send payment immediately?
If you trust the buyer's reputation, of course you can choose to send Monero sooner. But it's recommended you wait until the product has been delivered.

There are lots of ways to cheat with Amazon. The buyer can cancel the order before it's delivered. Amazon can recall the package if they detect fraud (if purchased with a stolen credit/gift card, or stolen account/password). It's best to wait until the product is in your hands.

How do I track my shipment?
Once the buyer posts the tracking number, you can track based on the shipping provider. I find "native" tracking works best. United States Postal Service, United Parcel Service, Federal Express, etc.

There are also 3rd party tracking sites to find shippers globally like AfterShip or Parcels

Amazon Tracking provides local tracking for items delivered by Amazon (trucks) if the tracking number resembles "TBA350490478526".

What if my order is damaged or incorrect?
Once your order is delievered, your transaction with the XMR Bazaar user is complete. If they ordered the correct item (from your wishlist), you should send their Monero.

If you ordered the wrong item, the item was damaged, has missing parts, or just don't like the item you received, you have to request a return from Amazon.

How do I return an item?
You should have a gift receipt in the box with instructions for returns via Amazon. If not start here https://www.amazon.com/returns. Amazon will return the "gift" and credit your account with the fiat amount of the purchase.

Earner Questions

Is there any KYC?
No! The shopper pays directly to your Monero address. It's a one-on-one transaction. There is no KYC. Amazon doesn't know you are receiving Monero for purchasing items on a wishlist.

Technically, Amazon could scrape XMR Bazaar for wishlist urls and deduce the purchase is in exchange for Monero.

Ideally, the wishlist url would be protected from public view on XMR Bazaar .

How can I get my Monero faster?
It is recommended that the shopper not send Monero until the item is delivered. Amazon can recall the item up to the day of delivery for various reasons (a canceled order, credit card fraud, stolen account, etc.).

If you choose a faster shipping option (next day / same day), it will be delivered sooner, meaning you'll get your Monero faster.

Of course, if you build a reputation, the shopper may trust that you won't cancel the order during shipment, and may choose to send Monero sooner.
